LeTip International

LeTip International, Inc. is the world's largest, privately owned, professional business leads organization. LeTip was founded in 1978 by Ken Peterson, an accomplished insurance executive who understood the powerful role that personal referrals play in the success of small business. Today, LeTip employs 15 Regional Directors who oversee more than 525 chapters throughout the USA and Canada. Peterson set the standard for referral organizations and LeTip members are known throughout the B2B referral industry for their dedication to the LeTip Program and helping one another grow their businesses. LeTip Chapters meet weekly to exchange qualified leads, build business relationships, develop strong presentation skills and become proficient networkers. Only one representative of any given profession is accepted into a chapter and members are chosen for their occupational expertise.
